Gardening updates

March 8, 2026

I've been having visions of a garden lately, not sure where this came from but I really want to grow vegetables indoors. But I started trying to re-grow kitchen scraps just to see what woud work. Some of it did and some of it didn't. I tried to regrow greens from the top of a carrot and greens from the bottom of an onion, but the carrot did nothing and the onion just kind of just wilted? But maybe I didn't wait long enough. I also tried to grow peppers from seeds I got from the grocery, which ended up molding, I think because I didn't dry the seeds and just threw them in straight from the pepper. Recently, I had success propagating dill stems from the grocery store, which I didn't think was going to work because it was cut off from the taproot, but there were a couple roots that grew after 5 weeks ?. I am going to plant it and see what happens... Also I threw two garlic cloves in a pot and they grew immediately, which was cool. I started growing basil from seed, and had some trouble germinating until I put the pot on top of the radiator and misted every day with plastic wrap on top so condensation could form.
